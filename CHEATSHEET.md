## Pro Tips

### 1. Combine Skills for Maximum Impact
- Freelancer Proposal + Digital Product Launcher = sell your services as products
- SaaS Idea to MVP + Revenue Dashboard = build and measure startups
- SEO Content Strategy + Email Marketing = warm leads at scale

### 2. Use Templates, Don't Memorize
Every skill includes templates, checklists, and examples. Copy them. Adapt them. Repeat.

### 3. Build Your Personal Library
When you adapt a template:
1. Save your version locally
2. Use as baseline for next project
3. Refine over time
4. Share with your team

### 4. Stack Skills for Your Role
Pick 3-5 skills matching your role and master them deeply. Master equals 50-100 hours of practice.

### 5. Track Your Progress
- [ ] Loaded 5 skills successfully
- [ ] Completed "Quick Win" for 3 skills
- [ ] Applied 1 skill to real work
- [ ] Saved 2 templates for reuse
- [ ] Shared a skill with someone

## FAQ

**Q: How do I load a skill?**
A: Run `/skills skill-name` in Claude Code.

**Q: Can I use multiple skills together?**
A: Yes! Many combinations work. See "Combine Skills" section above.

**Q: What if a skill doesn't fit my situation?**
A: Read the full skill for variations. Skills include edge cases and alternatives.

**Q: How long until I see results?**
A: Depends on the skill:
- Quick wins (< 1 hour): Invoice Generator, Resume Optimizer, Meeting to Actions
- Same-day (< 8 hours): Claude Code Power User, Analytics Setup
- Week 1 (< 1 week): SaaS MVP, Content Strategy, Email Campaigns
- Month 1 (< 1 month): Affiliate Site, Digital Product, Growth Playbook

**Q: Which skills should I learn first?**
A: Start with what you need RIGHT NOW. See "Find by Problem Type" above.

**Q: Can I learn multiple skills at once?**
A: Yes, but recommend mastering 1-2 before moving to 3+.
